Vaping Terminology

Vaping terminology refers to the language and specific terms used in the vaping community.

Some common terms include:

Vape:

the device used to vaporize e-liquid, also known as an electronic cigarette or e-cigarette

E-liquid:

the liquid that is vaporized and inhaled, also known as e-juice or vape juice

Atomizer:

the heating element in a vape that vaporizes the e-liquid

Coil:

the heating element in an atomizer, made of metal wire

Tank:

the container that holds the e-liquid

Mods:

short for modifications, refers to modified or custom vape devices

VG and PG:

the main ingredients in e-liquid, which stands for Vegetable Glycerin and Propylene Glycol

Nicotine:

a stimulant that is often added to e-liquid and is present in traditional cigarettes

Sub-ohm vaping:

vaping with an atomizer that has a resistance of less than 1 ohm

Cloud chasing:

the practice of exhaling large clouds of vapor
